Step 1: We are given two linear equations:

(1) x + y = 10
(2) x - y = 4

Step 2: Add equation (1) and (2) together to eliminate y:

(x + x) + (y - y) = 10 + 4
2x = 14

Step 3: Divide by 2 to solve for x:

x = 14 / 2
x = 7
